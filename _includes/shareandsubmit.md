## Share file from Google Drive and Submit Assignment
### In Google Drive
1. Find your file in Google Drive.
2. Right click on the file and select share.
3. Adjust the sharing General Access to `Christina School District`.
4. Click the `Copy Link` Button.

### In Schoology
1. Click `Submit Assignment`.
2. Go to the `Create` tab.
3. Insert `Inline Link`.
	* Paste the link to the Google Drive file in the `Link/URL` field.
	* In the `Text to Display` field, paste the Google Drive file.
4. Submit the Assignment.
