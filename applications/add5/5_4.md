# 5.4 | Final Export & Delivery: Packaging Your Podcast

The final stage of any production is "Delivery." This isn't just about finishing the edit; it's about exporting the correct file types and creating the visual branding (key art) that makes your podcast look professional on platforms like Spotify or YouTube.

## 1. Local Editing: The "Offline" Workflow
Before you can edit in Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve, you cannot work directly off the cloud. Working off a web browser causes lag and crashes.

* **Download your Assets:** Go to your Google Drive folder from Lesson 5.4.
* **Keep a Local Copy:** Right-click your files and select **Download**, or if using Google Drive Desktop, right-click and select **"Available Offline"** or **"Keep on this device."**
* **Edit:** Bring these local files into your timeline.

---

## 2. Master Export Settings
Once your edit is complete, you must export two "Master" versions: one for video platforms and one for audio-only platforms.

| Format | Codec/Type | Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Video** | **H.264 (.mp4)** | YouTube, Social Media, Portfolio |
| **Audio** | **WAV (.wav)** | Spotify, Apple Podcasts (High Quality) |



---

## 3. Creating the Visual Brand (Key Art)
A podcast needs "thumb-stopping" visuals. You must create two specific pieces of artwork using Photoshop or Canva.

### The Album Artwork
* **Dimensions:** 2400 x 2400 px (Square)
* **Format:** PNG
* **Content:** Must include the Podcast Title and high-contrast imagery.

### The YouTube Thumbnail
* **Dimensions:** 1920 x 1080 px (Widescreen)
* **Format:** PNG
* **Content:** Needs to be readable at a small size; use bold text and expressive faces.



---

## 4. Final Submission & Accountability
This is a group project, but **accountability is individual.** 1.  **Update your Folder:** Add your exported `.mp4`, `.wav`, and both `.png` art files to your shared Google Drive folder.
2.  **Every Member Submits:** Even if one person did the final export, **every group member** must copy the link and submit it to the LMS assignment.
3.  **No Link = No Grade:** If you do not submit the link under your own LMS account, the system will mark you as "Missing."

---

## 5. Delivery Checklist
Verify these items are in your folder before the deadline:

* [ ] **Video Master:** `Podcast_Final_H264.mp4`
* [ ] **Audio Master:** `Podcast_Final_HighQual.wav`
* [ ] **Square Art:** `Album_Art_2400.png`
* [ ] **Thumbnail:** `YouTube_Thumb_1080.png`
* [ ] **Access:** Is the link still set to "Anyone with the link can view"?
