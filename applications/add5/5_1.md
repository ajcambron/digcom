# 5.2 | Podcast Production Planner: AI in the Art World

In this project, you will transition from a consumer to a creator. You will research, script, and produce a **3–5 minute podcast** exploring the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and human creativity.

## 1. Defining Your Story
Before touching the equipment, you must define the scope of your broadcast.

* **The Driving Question:** What specific mystery or conflict about AI art are you trying to solve?
* **The "Not" List:** To stay under 5 minutes, what related topics will you intentionally exclude?
* **The Talent:** Identify your **Interviewer** (the host) and your **Guest** (the subject matter expert).

> ### Our Brief:
> Research and produce a 3 to 5 minute podcast exploring how Artificial Intelligence (AI) is influencing the art world. Your podcast should present a balanced and engaging discussion that informs listeners about the opportunities, challenges, and ethical questions surrounding AI-generated art.

---

## 2. Production Roles
A successful podcast requires a coordinated crew. Assign the following positions:

| Role | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Floor Director** | Manages the set, provides timing cues, and coordinates the crew. |
| **Video Engineer** | Operates the live switcher to change camera angles and graphics. |
| **Audio Engineer** | Monitors volume levels and triggers music or sound effects. |
